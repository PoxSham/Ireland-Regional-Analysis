Regional Output · NUTS3 · 2024

Where Output Is Concentrated

Six of Ireland's seven NUTS3 regions produce GVA per person below the national average of €99,513. Dublin and South-West account for over 55% of national output. The Border and Midlands lag at just 31% and 38% of the national average respectively.
