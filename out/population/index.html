Population, Migration, and Regional Divergence

Ireland's population is projected to grow from 5.18M (2022) to 5.78M by 2042, but growth is heavily concentrated in Dublin and the Mid-East. The Border region faces a decline of 11.4% over the same period, while Mid-East expands by 21.5%. Net migration reached +80,200 in 2024, with the east absorbing the majority of arrivals.
